CodeBits Company Limited

CodeBits is one of the few privately owned IT system integration, professional service, software, and hardware development companies in Uganda that works with Enterprise systems and companies. We are located in Ntinda-Trading center on Tuskys Building, offering fully-fledged services for software development and engineering empowering effective near-shore management to our clients.

At CodeBits, we develop innovative and creative products and services that provide total communication and information solutions. Among a plethora of services, hardware prototyping and development, web design and development, tailor-made applications, Enterprise resource planning (ERPs), Customer relationship management (CRMs), e-commerce solutions, business-to-business applications, business-to-client applications, managed hosting and internet portal management are few that we offer.

At CodeBits, our main aim is to assure the highest quality product, total client satisfaction, timely delivery of solutions and the best quality/price ratio found in the industry.

Our emphasis is on offering a high degree of product user-friendliness through a positive, creative and focused company staff.
